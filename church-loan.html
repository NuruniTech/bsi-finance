Financing for your ministry

BSI Finance supports churches, mosques, and faith-based organisations to build, expand, and serve their communities — with affordable construction financing and extended repayment periods.

Available to formally registered religious organisations across Tanzania — for construction, land purchase, equipment, or community programme financing.

Benefits of the Church & Ministry Loan

  • Our lowest interest rate — 14% per annum exclusively for faith-based organisations
  • Borrow up to TSh 300 million for large construction projects
  • Extended repayment terms up to 48 months — reducing monthly payment burden
  • Open to churches, mosques, and any formally constituted faith organisation
  • Repayment schedule aligned to congregation giving cycles on request
  • Construction-specialist advisor assigned for step-by-step project support
  • No early-repayment penalties
  • Funds disbursed in project stages to match construction progress

How we release funds

Phased Disbursement — Funds That Follow Your Build

Rather than releasing the full loan at once, BSI Finance can disburse funds in stages as your construction progresses. This protects your budget and ensures every shilling goes exactly where it should.

Stage 1
Foundation & Site Prep

First tranche released upon signed project contracts and confirmed site preparation works.

Stage 2
Structure & Walls

Second tranche released after foundation inspection and ground-floor walls are complete.

Stage 3
Roofing & Windows

Third tranche released once roofing is complete and windows are fitted.

Stage 4
Fit-Out & Completion

Final tranche released upon project completion and a final site inspection by our team.

The number of stages and percentage amounts are flexible and agreed with your advisor based on project size and type. We work around your construction plan, not the other way round.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Formally constituted and registered religious organisation — registration certificate required
  • Active organisational bank account — with at least 6 months of transaction history
  • Two formally appointed office-bearers — each with a valid NIDA ID or passport
  • Board or congregation resolution authorising the loan — signed and minuted
  • Property or land title collateral may be required for loans above TSh 20 million
  • No significant outstanding debts or pending legal claims against the organisation

Documents Required

Religious organisation registration certificate
Organisational bank statements for the past 6 months
Signed board or congregation resolution approving the loan
NIDA ID or passport copies for both authorised signatories
Land title deed or proof of collateral (for applicable loan amounts)
Completed BSI Finance application form
