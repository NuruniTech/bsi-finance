Eligibility Requirements

  • Formal employment of at least 6 months in a registered organisation
  • Employer must be registered with TRA and remitting PAYE
  • Net salary after deductions not less than TSh 100,000
  • Clean credit history — no loan defaults or arrears
  • Valid NIDA national ID or Tanzanian passport required
  • Employer must consent to direct payroll deduction

Documents Required

Letter of employment (official letterhead)
Last 3 months' payslips
Employer authorisation letter for payroll deduction
Copy of NIDA national ID or passport
2 recent passport-size photographs
Completed BSI Finance application form
