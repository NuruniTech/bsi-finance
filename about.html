Our story

Establishing trust, building futures.

BSI Finance Limited is a private-owned financial institution based in Tegeta, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Established to address the financial needs of underserved segments, BSI specialises in providing accessible small and medium-sized loans to entrepreneurs, religious institutions, salaried workers, and groups of individuals.

The institution is committed to responsible lending practices, financial inclusion, and poverty reduction through affordable, tailored credit products. Every product carries transparent rates, no hidden fees, and a fast 48-hour decision commitment.

Licensed and regulated by the Bank of Tanzania
Over TSh 18 billion disbursed since inception
97% on-time repayment rate across all portfolios

Our Vision

To be an excellent financial service institution promoting financial inclusion and serving all clients across Tanzania, especially farmers, business enterprises, workers, and religious institutions.

Our Mission

To contribute to the economic and social development of Tanzanians by developing specialised financial products that advance financial inclusion, empower entrepreneurs, support churches, and provide affordable credit solutions to reduce poverty.
