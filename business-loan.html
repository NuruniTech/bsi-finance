Check our minimum requirements:

Are we a match?

  • Business registered with BRELA or TRA — operating for at least 12 months
  • Valid TIN number and 6 months of business bank transaction records
  • Director or principal owner with valid NIDA ID or Tanzanian passport
  • Clean credit history — no significant outstanding loan defaults
  • Collateral may be required for loans above TSh 10 million
  • Applicant must be a Tanzanian citizen or permanent resident
Tip for success: When applying for a loan, it's recommended that businesses borrow no more than 20–30% of annual revenue to maintain healthy cash flow.

What documents will I need?

  • Business registration certificate (BRELA) or valid trade licence
  • Business bank statements for the last 6 months
  • Annual financial statements — balance sheet and profit/loss accounts
  • Copy of owner/director's NIDA ID or passport
  • Proof of collateral (title deed or vehicle documents) where applicable
  • Completed BSI Finance application form
Good to know: With all documents submitted in full, our assessment typically takes under 72 hours.

How to Apply for Financing

  • Step 1: Contact our team or visit our Tegeta office — your advisor will walk you through the options
  • Step 2: Submit your business documents — our team conducts a thorough business assessment
  • Step 3: Receive your loan offer within 72 hours and select the structure that works for you
  • Step 4: Funds are disbursed directly to your registered business bank account
